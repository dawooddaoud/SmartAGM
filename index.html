Virtual Annual General Assembly gives you the opportunity to attend the AGM remotely, participate and vote in real-time, using your smartphone, tablet or computer

TO REGISTER FOR THE ANNUAL MEETING

Step 1
Click on the Company's logo you wish to attend
Step 2
Fill out the registration application and attach the required documents
Step 3
Once the application gets approved a confirmation email will be sent to you including the meeting ID, Username, and Password to the email and mobile number provided in the applciation form 24 hours before the date of the Annual General Meeting
Step 4
You will also be provided with a user guide to attend the General Assembly remotly.
